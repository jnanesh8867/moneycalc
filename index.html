Frequently Asked Questions

How is EMI calculated in India? +
EMI = [P × R × (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N - 1]. P = Principal amount, R = Monthly interest rate, N = Number of months. Our calculator uses this RBI-approved formula for accurate results.
What is SIP and how does compounding work? +
SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) lets you invest a fixed amount monthly in mutual funds. Compounding means your returns also earn returns. ₹5,000/month at 12% for 20 years = ₹49.9 lakhs from just ₹12 lakhs invested!
Which tax regime is better in 2025-26? +
For income below ₹7 lakhs — New Regime (zero tax with rebate u/s 87A). For higher incomes with large deductions (80C, HRA, home loan interest), Old Regime may save more. Use our calculator to compare both instantly.
How to calculate GST in India 2026? +
GST Amount = Base Price × Rate/100. For intra-state: split into CGST + SGST equally. For inter-state: IGST. Our calculator handles all cases automatically.
How much take-home from ₹10 LPA CTC? +
At ₹10 LPA CTC, monthly take-home is approximately ₹70,000–₹75,000 after PF, TDS, and professional tax deductions. Use our salary calculator for exact amounts based on your state and PF contribution.
